Our Startup Journey

From students to tech leaders, our path of continuous improvement

2025 - The Beginning

Two Students, One Vision

WEDOXA started as a passion project between two tech students. While pursuing our degrees, we recognized the gap between academic learning and real-world application.

Our mission was clear: Create a platform where we could apply classroom knowledge to solve real problems while building our skills portfolio.

2025 October - First Breakthrough

Lagan Bus Booking System

Our first major project addressed a real community need - modernizing local bus transportation. This became our proving ground, combining AI concepts with practical web development.

The project taught us project management, client communication, and the importance of user-centered design - lessons no classroom could fully provide.

Present - Dual Leadership

Leaders & Lifelong Learners

Today, we lead WEDOXA while continuing our academic pursuits. This unique position gives us both theoretical depth from our studies and practical experience from our projects.

Student by day, innovator by passion

Our Learning Philosophy

We believe the best education happens at the intersection of theory and practice. Every project is both a service to our clients and a lesson for our growth.

We Start. We Learn. We Improve.
